Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 March 2026

Ringback ("we", "our", "us") is operated by Nixflex Enterprises LLC. This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use the Ringback application and services.

Ringback is an AI receptionist service. To provide this service, call data is processed using secure third-party AI and telephony providers. We are transparent about what data is collected and how it is used.

1. Information we collect

Account information

When you create an account, we collect your email address, business name, your name, and business details you choose to provide (such as address, services offered, and working hours).

Call data

When a caller reaches your AI receptionist, we process and store:

  • Caller's phone number
  • Call audio recording
  • Call transcript
  • AI-generated call summary
  • Call duration and timestamp
  • Language detected during the call

Booking data

If you enable appointment booking, we store appointment details including service, date, time, and the caller's contact information.

Payment information

Subscription payments are processed by a secure third-party payment processor. We store your subscription status but never store your card number, CVV, or full payment details.

Device information

We collect your device's push notification token to send you call alerts. We do not track your location, browsing activity, or collect device analytics.

2. How we use your information

We use your data to:

  • Provide the AI receptionist service — answering calls, generating summaries, and booking appointments
  • Send you notifications when new calls are received
  • Send follow-up text messages to callers on your behalf
  • Process your subscription payments
  • Improve and maintain the service

3. Third-party data processing

To deliver the AI receptionist service, your data is processed by trusted third-party service providers in the following categories. By using Ringback, you consent to this processing.

We work with carefully selected service providers who process data on our behalf:

  • AI voice and language processing — to power real-time voice conversations with callers and understand what they need
  • Voice synthesis — to generate natural-sounding speech for your AI receptionist
  • Telephony services — to provide phone numbers, route calls, and send SMS messages
  • Cloud database services — to securely store your account data, call records, and business information
  • Payment processing — to handle subscription billing securely

All third-party providers are contractually bound to protect your data and use it only for the purposes of delivering our service. We do not share your data with any parties beyond what is necessary to operate Ringback.

4. Call recording

All calls handled by your AI receptionist are recorded and transcribed to provide you with call summaries and records. As the business owner using Ringback, you are responsible for complying with local call recording laws in your jurisdiction.

We recommend informing your callers that calls may be recorded, in line with local regulations. In the UK, businesses may record calls for legitimate business purposes under applicable telecommunications regulations.

5. What we do NOT do

  • We do not sell your personal data or call data to anyone
  • We do not use your data for advertising or marketing by third parties
  • We do not use your call recordings to train AI models
  • We do not share your data beyond what is described in this policy
  • We do not collect or store caller payment information

6. Data security

Your data is protected with industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit and at rest, secure authentication, and access controls. Only you can access your call recordings and business data through the Ringback app.

7. Data retention and deletion

We retain your data for as long as your account is active so you can access your full call history at any time.

You can delete your account at any time from the app (Profile > Delete Account). When you delete your account, we permanently and irreversibly delete:

  • Your account and all business information
  • All call records, transcripts, and recordings
  • All customer records and booking data
  • Your assigned phone number
  • Your subscription

8. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction — correct inaccurate data (editable directly in the app)
  • Deletion — delete your account and all data (available in the app)
  • Portability — request your data in a portable format
  • Objection — object to certain processing of your data
  • Restriction — request limits on how we use your data

These rights apply under the UK GDPR, EU GDPR, and other applicable data protection laws. To exercise any right not available directly in the app, contact us at support@nixflex.com.

9. Children's privacy

Ringback is a business tool and is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes through the app or via email. Continued use of Ringback after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
